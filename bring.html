It's important to keep in mind the length of your stay at any one of these campgrounds. While thankfully none require any specialized equipment such as climbing tools, an overnight trip will demand more supplies than a day hike. Items in the one day list also apply to the several days list

One Day

  • adequate footwear
  • water
  • adequate clothing
  • some means of self defense like bear spray
  • compass
  • first aid kit
  • bug repellent

Several Days

  • bear can (if need be)
  • food, preferably freezedried since they last longer and don't smell as much
  • tent
  • firestarter or portable stove depending on campground rules
  • hatchet
  • light source, ideally a headlamp as it leaves your hands free
  • backpack
Make sure to always pay attention to trailhead bulletins for information regarding burn bans and bear activity!
